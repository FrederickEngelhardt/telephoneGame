About Us

This game is based off perception. You write a story and someone creates
their iterpratation of it in visual form. This process repeats and we get to
see how much your story has changed as it gets further and further misinterpreted!
This was inspired by the telephone game where a group of people sit in a circle
and attempt to keep the orginal message intact and when this inevitable fails try
to find out where the message was corrupted.
